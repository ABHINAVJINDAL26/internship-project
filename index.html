SEASON AND ARCS

Step into the haunting world of Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba Season 1, where tragedy sparks an unbreakable resolve. After Tanjiro Kamado's family is brutally slain by demons and his sister Nezuko is cursed to live as one, he embarks on a relentless journey to restore her humanity.

The Mugen Train Arc ignites the next chapter of Tanjiro's journey as he, Nezuko, Zenitsu, and Inosuke step aboard the eerie Infinity Train, where over forty passengers have mysteriously vanished.
The Entertainment District Arc thrusts Tanjiro, Zenitsu, Inosuke, and Nezuko into the dazzling yet dangerous world of Yoshiwara's red-light district.
The Swordsmith Village Arc carries Tanjiro to the hidden sanctuary where the master swordsmiths forge the blades that keep Demon Slayers alive.
The Hashira Training Arc marks a turning point for the Demon Slayer Corps as the looming battle against Muzan Kibutsuji draws ever closer.

Tanjiro Kamado is the courageous and compassionate protagonist of Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba.

Zenitsu Agatsuma brings both comedy and incredible power to the Demon Slayer Corps.
